Log Explorer (just list the jobs + fields)

Filter (paste into Log Explorer > Query):

resource.type="bigquery_project"
logName="projects/PROJECT_ID/logs/cloudaudit.googleapis.com%2Fdata_access"
protoPayload.metadata."@type"="type.googleapis.com/google.cloud.audit.BigQueryAuditMetadata"
protoPayload.metadata.jobChange.after="DONE"
protoPayload.metadata.jobStats.queryStats.totalBilledBytes:* 
timestamp >= "NOW-24H"


After running it, click “Table” view and add these fields as columns:

timestamp

protoPayload.authenticationInfo.principalEmail

protoPayload.metadata.jobChange.job.jobName.jobId

protoPayload.metadata.jobChange.job.jobConfig.queryConfig.query (the SQL)

protoPayload.metadata.jobStats.queryStats.totalProcessedBytes

protoPayload.metadata.jobStats.queryStats.totalBilledBytes

These fields come from the BigQueryAuditMetadata payload in Logging and include both totalProcessedBytes and totalBilledBytes for query jobs
